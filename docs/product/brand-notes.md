## Purpose

This document captures lightweight brand and communication direction before a full brand identity system exists.

## Brand Direction

SquadSync should feel:

- practical
- organized
- coach-centered
- trustworthy
- calm under game-day pressure
- focused on helping teams prepare and communicate better

## Voice and Tone

Use clear, direct language.

Prefer soccer-specific terms when they help clarity. Avoid generic enterprise language when a simple coaching term is better.

## Visual Direction

No visual identity has been selected yet.

Future visual work may define:

- logo direction
- color palette
- typography
- spacing system
- icon usage
- dashboard visual style

## Current Rule

Do not introduce visual assets or strong brand commitments before the web app direction is clearer.
