## Purpose

This document captures the lightweight product direction for SquadSync before detailed product requirements or UX specifications exist.

## Product Summary

SquadSync is a soccer-focused team management and match-planning platform for coaches.

The MVP should help a coach manage team context, roster information, match planning, availability, and manual lineup construction.

## Target User

Initial target user:

- youth soccer coach
- small club or team manager
- needs practical planning support without enterprise sports-management complexity

## MVP Problem

Coaches often track rosters, availability, match plans, and lineups across disconnected tools, memory, spreadsheets, texts, and ad hoc notes.

SquadSync should provide a focused workflow for managing the core team/match planning context in one place.

## MVP Product Direction

The MVP focuses on:

- team creation
- roster management
- player/coach profiles
- membership roles
- match planning
- player availability
- manual lineup construction
- future service boundary for lineup assistance

## Non-Goals

The MVP does not include:

- full club administration
- payments
- recruiting workflows
- complex league management
- broad multi-sport competition catalog behavior
- embedded soccer-subber optimization logic

## Future Product Areas

Potential future areas:

- soccer-subber lineup assistance integration
- event-driven notifications
- AI-generated planning summaries
- richer web dashboard UX
- cloud-hosted demo environment
